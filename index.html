Welcome

Welcome to Green Futures with Neha, a blog (that I coded using HTML and CSS) where I share my thoughts on growing, learning, and taking small steps toward a more sustainable future. I’m a high school student from New York, and I’ve become really interested in environmental science because of how clearly issues like global warming are affecting the world right now. It doesn’t feel like something far away anymore. It’s already shaping our everyday lives and will continue to shape our future. That’s part of why I care about this topic so much.

Through this blog, I want to learn more about environmental science, explore real-world environmental challenges, and share what I discover along the way. Whether I’m writing about climate change, ecosystems, conservation, renewable energy, or simple ways to live more sustainably, I hope this blog reminds you that even small actions can make a difference.

I hope you can join me on this journey. From adventures, to volunteering experiences, to research I come across, or even books I read, I’ll be sharing it all here as I learn and grow.
